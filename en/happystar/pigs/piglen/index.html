The place in Piglen where the toy pigs live will eventually be made into a virtual world. Right now, the Little Pink Pigs' mushroom houses and Yummy Little Piggy's gingerbread house have partially been made.

Piglen will be made using THREE.js, tween.js, and pictures found on the internet.

Good and Evil pigs

Pigs can be classified as "good" or "evil". Most good pigs live in Piglen, and most evil pigs live in Evil Piglen. Evil Piglen is far away from good Piglen, so most evil pigs have not come to good Piglen yet. Most good pigs have an evil counterpart.

Evil pigs can eat both light and other pigs. Evil pigs are generally more powerful than good pigs. When an evil pigs eats/defeats a pig, the evil pig will become even more powerful, especially when eating another evil pig. If a good pig eats an evil pig, the good pig may become evil! This likely won't happen, however, when the difference in size or power is quite large. For example, sun pig is unlikely to be affected by eating ice pig.
