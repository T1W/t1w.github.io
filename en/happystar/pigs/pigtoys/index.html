Piggy Toys

Big Pig

Big Pig is a very big squishable pig made out of polyester! Big Pig is very warm and soft. Big Pig is a very nice pig toy who likes to eat many kinds of foods! Big Pig's favorite foods are New Pig, ham, lychee, crab, and watermelon! Big Pig has learned a lot of math from New Pig before! Big Pig likes to play with people!

Big Pig likes to travel to some places to eat and play and likes to cook food too. Big Pig likes to bounce and roll! Big Pig likes playing with and riding on FlyPig.

FlyPig

FlyPig is a mini squishables piggy made of polyester! FlyPig is warm and soft like Big Pig. FlyPig has a darker color than Big Pig. FlyPig has designs for ears, legs, eyes, and a nose different from the ones on Big Pig. FlyPig likes to eat foods such as birds, pigs, fish, and lychee. FlyPig also likes eating New Pig. FlyPig likes to fly!

FlyPig likes sleeping and playing with Big Pig and people.

New Pig

New Pig is a Bright Starts Pretty and Pink Pull and Play Pig! New Pig likes eating many kinds of foods! New Pig is a hyperpig and has a diamond. New Pig is mostly pink and has a bow with dots on it. There are multiple textures on some parts of New Pig's body. New Pig likes to sleep on a bed with other little pink pigs.

The other little pink pigs are Little Pink Sister, Little Pink Pig, and Little Pink Brother!

Yummy Little Piggy and Our Pig

Yummy and Our Pig are blue mushabelly pigs. They were bought near the year 2011. Yummy the mushabelly pig is the most delicious piggy toy! The little pink pigs like to eat these pigs!
